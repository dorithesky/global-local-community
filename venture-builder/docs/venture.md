# Venture: PricePage Pulse

## Thesis
Small B2B SaaS teams constantly ship pricing page changes without noticing competitor changes. Founders and growth leads will pay for a lightweight competitor pricing change alert service faster than they will adopt a large platform.

## Niche
Bootstrapped and seed-stage B2B SaaS founders tracking 3-10 competitors.

## Problem
Competitor pricing, packaging, and CTA changes directly impact win rates, but most teams track them manually or not at all.

## Offer
Weekly competitor pricing page monitoring with instant change alerts and summary diffs.

## Fastest path to first $1
1. Build a landing page with a paid pre-order / manual concierge onboarding.
2. Collect leads immediately.
3. Deliver the first reports manually with lightweight automation behind the scenes.

## Monetization
- Founding plan: $9 one-time setup + $19/month for up to 5 competitors.
- Early concierge option: $29 manual setup done-for-you.

## Go-to-market
- Direct outreach to indie hackers and small SaaS founders.
- X account content around pricing teardown/change alerts.
- Launch page with email capture and payment CTA.

## Tech stack
- Next.js static landing page
- Simple JSON-backed lead storage in workspace for now
- OpenClaw cron for daily status/reporting and later monitoring jobs
- AgentMail for outreach and lead follow-up
- Future: Stripe payment link, screenshot/diff worker, Supabase or SQLite

## Success metric
First paid founding customer as quickly as possible, ideally via concierge sale before full automation.

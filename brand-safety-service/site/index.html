Brand Guard & Domain Scout

Automated brand-name screening for founders. It checks trademark conflicts, fuzzy/phonetic similarity, and domain availability across core startup extensions.

Trademark screening
Direct match plus likelihood-of-confusion heuristics.
Domain lookup
Checks .com, .ai, .io, and .co.
Launch report
Returns risk level, availability map, and alternative suggestions.
